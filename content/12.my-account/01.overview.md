**My Account** is your personal corner of DevStride. It's where *you* manage *your own* settings — distinct from **Configure Settings**, which is for organization-wide configuration that admins control.

A simple way to tell them apart:

- **My Account** — *your* profile, security, notifications, API keys, and connected tools. Available to every member.
- **Configure Settings** — *the organization's* work model, people, roles, billing, and integrations. Available to admins.

## What's in My Account

| Area | What you do there |
| --- | --- |
| **Profile** | Your name, contact details, and personal preferences. |
| **Organizations** | The DevStride organizations you belong to, and switching between them. |
| **Multi-Factor Authentication** | Turn on and manage MFA to secure your sign-in. |
| **Notification preferences** | Choose what you're notified about and how. |
| **API Keys** | Create and manage personal keys for programmatic access. |
| **Connect AI / Connected Apps** | Connect AI assistants and other tools to your account. |

## Documented in detail

- [**API Keys**](/my-account/api-keys) — create a key, the one-time secret, and (for admins) org-wide key management.
- [**Notification preferences**](/notifications/overview-of-notification-options) — tailor what reaches you and where.
- [**Multi-Factor Authentication**](/settings/organization/users-roles-and-mfa) — setting up MFA (and, for admins, resetting it for others).
- [**Connect AI and the MCP server**](/settings/integrations/connect-ai-and-mcp) — connect an AI assistant; see also the [DevStride MCP Server](/devstride-mcp-server/what-is-the-devstride-mcp-server) section.

::alert{type="note" title="Finding My Account in the app"}
Open **My Account** from your account menu. It sits alongside **Configure Settings** but contains only *your* personal settings — changes here affect your experience, not the whole organization.
::
