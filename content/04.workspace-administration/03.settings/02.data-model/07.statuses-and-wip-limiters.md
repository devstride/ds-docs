### 1 - Create Status

To create a status, click the green "New" button next to 1 - Create Status. Statuses New and Done are reserved by the system and cannot be changed, while other statuses are user-defined. Examples of user-defined statuses include:

Approved
In Progress
Pending Release
Review
Review Req

### 2 - Group into Collections

Once you've created statuses, you can group them into collections, and set a WIP Limiter for each status. WIP stands for Work in Progress, and a WIP Limiter is a tool used to control the amount of work that is currently being handled in a production process, project, or workflow.

### 3 - Assign Collection to Teams

You can assign collections of statuses and WIP limiters to various team within your organization. Leave it to default, or assign a "simple" collection containing the following statuses:

New
In Progress
Done
For more elaborate collections, click the dropdown menu under Collections to manually assign a status collection to each team.

### Deleting a status

Deleting a status first relocates every item still in it to the first valid status in the item's collection, so nothing is left pointing at a status that no longer exists. Two rules apply:

- The system statuses **New** and **Done** cannot be deleted.
- A status holding **more than 1,000 items** cannot be deleted in one step — DevStride refuses with a clear message rather than attempting a relocation too large to complete safely. Move or drain the status below that limit, then delete it.
