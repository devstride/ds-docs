Switch the view. See the week the way that works for you.

Weekly Logs gives you two ways to look at the same week: **List** and **Grouped**. The view toggle sits in the toolbar.

## List view

**List view** is the default. Tasks are laid out in a flat list, grouped into two sections — **Items You Logged This Week** at the top, and **Proposed items** below. It is straightforward and works well when you want to see everything in one place and move through your timesheet quickly.

## Grouped view

**Grouped view** organises the same tasks by workstream. If your work spans multiple folders, grouped view clusters tasks under their workstream path, so you can see where your time is going across different areas of work at a glance.

## When to use each view

Switch based on what helps you work fastest. Use **list** view when you want a flat timesheet and inline actions such as linking calendar events to tasks. Use **grouped** view when you want time broken down by workstream path.
