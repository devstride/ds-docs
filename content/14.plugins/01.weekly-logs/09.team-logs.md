**Team Logs** is for admins who need to view and manage time entries across the entire team — not just their own.

## Turn on Team Logs

1. In the toolbar, toggle **Team Logs** on.
2. The grid updates immediately to multi-user mode.

Switching **Team Logs** on or off clears workstream filter tags so the new scope does not keep stale filters.

When **Team Logs** is on, the **Proposed items** section is not shown. Use your own timesheet (Team Logs off) for suggested work tabs.

## Expandable tasks and rows

In Team Logs, tasks are still the rows — but each task is **expandable**.

1. Click the **chevron** on a task to show every team member who has logged time against it.
2. Each person gets **their own row**: hours per day and a weekly total for that person.
3. The **task row** shows the **combined total** across everyone who logged on that task.

You can expand and collapse tasks one at a time, or use **Expand All** and **Collapse All** in the header to open or close every task at once.

## Edit and add entries

As an admin, you can **edit anyone's entries** directly when your organization allows it: click a user's cell, enter the hours, and it saves like any other entry.

You can **add a team member to a task** by clicking the **+** button on the task row and choosing a user from the list.

## Assignee filter (Team Logs only)

To focus on specific people, use the **Assignee** filter — **available only in Team Logs mode**.

1. Open **Filters**, then **Assignee**.
2. Select one or more team members.
3. Click **Apply**.

The grid narrows to those users and the tasks they've logged time on.

For how filters combine with other options, see [Filtering](/plugins/weekly-logs/filtering).
