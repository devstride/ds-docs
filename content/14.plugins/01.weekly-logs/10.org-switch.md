When you belong to **more than one organization**, time tracking can get complicated: work is split across teams and projects, but your week still has the same hours. Logging in one org without losing sight of what you logged elsewhere is easy to get wrong — and reconciling totals across orgs by the end of the week can turn into a manual exercise.

Weekly Logs addresses this with the **org switcher** and the **All Orgs** view.

## Org selector in the toolbar

On the **right side of the toolbar**, an **org selector** shows your currently active organization.

1. Click it to open the dropdown.
2. Every organization you are a member of is listed there.

**Switching is instant.** Choose a different org and the grid reloads with that organization's tasks, items, and team.

- Any **unsaved changes** in the current org are **saved automatically** before the switch completes.
- **Custom fields**, **teams**, **folder structure**, and **available items** are org-specific — the grid updates to match the org you selected.

## All Orgs view

At the **top of the dropdown** there is an **All Orgs** option (marked **read-only**).

1. Select **All Orgs** to see a **combined** view of your time entries across **every** organization you belong to.
2. Tasks are **grouped by organization name**, so you get one picture of your whole week across every team you are part of.
3. Use this to check that hours are balanced and nothing is missing.

In **All Orgs** mode, **editing** and **exporting** are **disabled**. It is a **view-only summary**. To change entries or run an export, switch back to a **specific organization**.
