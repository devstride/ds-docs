Finding the right work should not slow you down. Weekly Logs gives you two ways to add items to your timesheet: a quick keyword search for when you know what you are looking for, and an advanced hierarchy browser for when you need to explore.

## Keyword search

The search bar sits in the toolbar.

1. Type at least two characters — a ticket number, a title, or a keyword.
2. Results appear immediately. Items already in your timesheet are marked.
3. Click a result to add it to the grid, ready for a time entry.

## Narrow by workstream

Before searching, you can narrow the scope using the workstream filter next to the search bar. Select a workstream, and results are limited to items within that folder.

## Advanced search

For times when you need to browse rather than search, use the advanced search button — the folder icon next to the search bar. This opens the hierarchy browser.

1. On the left, browse your full item hierarchy — workstreams and work items.
2. Navigate into a folder using the breadcrumb.
3. Select the items that belong in your week.
4. Click **Add Selected** to add all selected items to the grid at once.

For suggested work you do not need to search for, see [Proposed items](/plugins/weekly-logs/proposed-items).
