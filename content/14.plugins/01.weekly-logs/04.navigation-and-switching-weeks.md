![Weekly Logs week navigation and date picker](/images/weekly-logs/navigation.png)

Weekly Logs gives you two ways to navigate between weeks: arrow controls for stepping one week at a time, and a date picker for jumping directly to any point in the year.

## Step through weeks

The current week is always visible in the toolbar, displayed as a date range — for example, **May 11 – May 17, 2026**.

- Click the **left arrow** to go to the previous week.
- Click the **right arrow** to go to the next week.

Each click shifts the grid by one week and the date range updates to match.

## Jump to any week

When you need to navigate further — to a past week for a correction, or a future week to plan ahead — click the **date range** in the toolbar to open the date picker.

1. The calendar opens on the current month.
2. Use the arrow buttons to navigate between months, or step through years to go further back or forward.
3. You can also type a date directly into the input field in **DD/MM/YYYY** format — the calendar jumps to that month automatically.
4. Click any date. The grid moves to the start of that week.

::alert{title="Week anchoring" type="info"}
Weekly Logs always anchors to the full work week. Any date you select lands you on the Monday of that week — not the specific day you clicked.
::

## Return to the current week

If you have navigated away from the current week, click **Today** inside the date picker to jump straight back.
