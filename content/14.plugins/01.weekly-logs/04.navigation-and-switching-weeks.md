![Weekly Logs week navigation and date picker](/images/weekly-logs/navigation.png)

Moving through the timesheet should be fast and precise. Weekly Logs gives you two ways to navigate: quick arrow controls for stepping through weeks, and a date picker for jumping directly to any point in the year.

## Step one week at a time

The current week is always visible in the toolbar, displayed as a date range — for example, **May 11 – May 17, 2026**. Use the arrow buttons on either side to step backward or forward one week at a time. Each click shifts the grid to the next or previous week, and the date range updates to match.

## Jump to any week

When you need to jump further — to a past week for a correction, or a future week to plan ahead — click the date range itself to open the date picker.

The calendar opens with the current month in view. Navigate between months using the arrow buttons, or step through years if you need to go further back. You can also type a date directly into the input field in **DD/MM/YYYY** format, and the calendar will jump to that month automatically.

Click any date and the grid moves to the start of that week. Weekly Logs always anchors to the full work week, so any day you select lands you on the right starting point.

If you have navigated away from the current week, click **Today** inside the date picker to jump straight back.
