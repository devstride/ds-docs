---
title: Logging
description: Enter hours, add notes, and remove time entries in Weekly Logs.
---

![Weekly Logs time entry](/images/weekly-logs/logging.png)

This is where time tracking actually happens. Each row in the grid is a task. Each column is a day of the week.

## Enter time

1. Find the task row — added from [Search](/plugins/weekly-logs/search) or [Proposed items](/plugins/weekly-logs/proposed-items).
2. Click the cell for the day you want to log against.
3. Enter your hours. Decimals are supported — **1.5** for 1 hour 30 minutes, **2.25** for 2 hours 15 minutes, and so on.
4. Move to the next cell. You do not need to hit Save — entries are saved automatically.

Hours are rounded to the nearest quarter automatically. You can log against multiple days for the same task by entering hours in each day's cell independently.

::alert{title="Maximum hours" type="info"}
A single entry cannot exceed **24 hours** per day. Values outside this range will not be accepted.
::

## Add notes

Every time entry can have a note attached to it.

1. Hover over a filled cell — a notes icon appears.
2. Click the notes icon, or press **Space Bar** while the cell is active.
3. In the popup, add a description alongside the hours and save.

The note stays attached to that specific entry on that specific day. If a note exists, the icon remains visible on the cell so you can identify entries with context at a glance.

## Remove time

- To remove a **single entry**, clear the cell or set it to **0**. Weekly Logs will ask you to confirm before deleting.
- To delete **all entries for a task** across the entire week, click the delete icon in the task's total cell and confirm the deletion.

::alert{title="Notes are cleared on delete" type="info"}
When a time entry is deleted, any note attached to it is removed as well.
::
