This is where time tracking actually happens. Each row in the grid is a task. Each column is a day of the week.

## Enter time

1. Find the task row (added from [Search](/plugins/weekly-logs/search) or [Proposed items](/plugins/weekly-logs/proposed-items)).
2. Click the cell for the day you want.
3. Enter your hours. Decimals are supported — **1.5** for 1 hour 30 minutes, **2.25** for 2 hours 15 minutes, and so on. Hours are rounded to the nearest quarter automatically.
4. Continue to the next cell. You do not need to hit Save — entries are saved automatically.

You can log against multiple days for the same task by entering hours in each day's cell.

## Add notes

Need more context on an entry?

1. Click the notes icon inside the cell, or press **Space Bar** while the cell is active.
2. In the popup, add a description and save.

The note stays attached to that specific entry on that specific day.

## Log time from a calendar event or Teams call

In **list** view, on **Calendar** or **Calls** rows, use the inline add flow on the row to pick a DevStride task (search works like the main toolbar). When a recurring event is merged across multiple days on one row, use the day checkboxes so the add applies to the days you intend, then choose the task. Time is added to your timesheet on the linked task for the selected days.

In **grouped** view, use those tabs to review events and calls; switch to **list** view to link them to tasks from the row.

## Remove time

- To remove a single entry, clear the cell or set it to **0**.
- To delete all entries for a task across the entire week, click the delete icon in the task's total cell and confirm the deletion.
