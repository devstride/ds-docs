## Overview

![Weekly Logs overview](/images/weekly-logs/overview.png)

Weekly Logs is a time-tracking plugin built directly inside DevStride. Because it lives where the work already lives, there is no context switch — your tickets are already there, your week is already laid out, and logging time is a matter of finding the task and entering the hours.

The plugin is built around a weekly grid. Work assigned to you surfaces automatically. Entries save as you go. And every hour stays attached to the exact task it came from — not a free-text description, not a spreadsheet row, but the actual item your team is tracking.

## Open Weekly Logs

1. Open the **Plugins** section from the menu.
2. Choose **Weekly Logs**.

The Weekly Logs interface loads in the application. The plugin uses the same authentication as DevStride — you do not sign in again separately, and your user and organization are loaded automatically.

If something goes wrong, refresh the page. If the session may have expired, sign out and sign back in.

## What you can do

- Log time in a weekly grid with automatic save
- Switch between **list** and **grouped** views
- Add tasks with search or advanced hierarchy browse
- Use **Proposed items** for suggested work
- Turn on **Team Logs** to manage multiple users when permitted
- Filter, export to CSV, and switch organizations when you belong to more than one
