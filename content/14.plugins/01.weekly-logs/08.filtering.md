Your timesheet can grow long quickly. Filters help you cut through the noise and focus on what matters right now.

Weekly Logs has a **filter panel** that narrows what is visible in the grid. Click **Filters** in the toolbar to open it.

## Items

Search by **item number** or **name**. Type a number (or search text), press **Enter**, and only rows matching that item stay visible. Useful when you know exactly what you are looking for.

## Workstream

Filter by **folder** or **hierarchy path**. If your tasks are organized into workstreams, you can focus on one area at a time and hide everything outside it.

## Team

Filter by **team assignment**. If your organization has multiple teams, use this to see only tasks that belong to a specific team.

## Assignee

**Assignee** is available in **Team Logs** mode. Pick one or more team members and the grid shows only their rows — handy for reviewing one person’s week without the rest of the team in view. See [Team Logs](/plugins/weekly-logs/team-logs).

## Organizations

**Organizations** is available in **All Orgs** mode. When you are looking across multiple organizations, use it to filter down to a **specific organization**. See [Org switch](/plugins/weekly-logs/org-switch).

## Combine filters and clear them

You can combine any of these filters. **Each filter you add narrows the grid further** — results must match **all** of your active filters (logical **AND**).

- **Within one filter type**, when you select multiple values (for example several assignees), the grid usually includes rows that match **any** of those selections (**OR** within that type).

**Active filters** appear as **chips above the grid**. Click **X** on a chip to remove that filter, or use **Clear All** to reset everything at once.

Use filters when the grid gets long and you need a specific slice of work — see less, work faster.
