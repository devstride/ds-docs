![Weekly Logs grid](/images/weekly-logs/grid.png)

The grid is the core of Weekly Logs. Every task you are tracking sits as a row, and every day of the week is a column — Monday through Sunday, with **Total** on the right. At the bottom, a pinned **Total** row shows the sum of every day and your full week at a glance.

## Toolbar and header

Above the grid, the plugin title, status messages (for example when changes are saving), and **Export** appear in the header row.

The main controls sit in a bordered **toolbar** strip:

- You can **collapse** the toolbar to save space (collapse control on the right, or **Ctrl+Shift+F** / **Cmd+Shift+F**).
- When collapsed, a **Toolbar** control appears next to **Export** so you can expand it again.
- Active filters appear as chips above the grid when filters are applied.

View mode, search, week navigation, filters, **Team Logs**, and **Export** are covered in their own sections: [Views](/plugins/weekly-logs/views), [Search](/plugins/weekly-logs/search), [Navigation & switching weeks](/plugins/weekly-logs/navigation-and-switching-weeks), [Filtering](/plugins/weekly-logs/filtering), [Team Logs](/plugins/weekly-logs/team-logs), and [Export](/plugins/weekly-logs/export).

## Grid sections

The grid is divided into two sections. Each section is collapsible, so you can focus on what matters at any point.

- **Items You Logged This Week** — Everything you have already entered hours against.
- **Proposed items** — Suggested work assigned to you or carried over from previous weeks. See [Proposed items](/plugins/weekly-logs/proposed-items).

## Cells and entries

Each cell represents one day on one task. Click a cell to enter hours.

- **Today** is highlighted in the column headers.
- Use the notes button on a cell to add a description alongside the hours.
- Every hour you enter stays attached to the task it belongs to.
