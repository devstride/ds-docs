![Weekly Logs grid](/images/weekly-logs/grid.png)

The grid is the core of Weekly Logs. Every task you are tracking sits as a row, and every day of the week is a column — Monday through Sunday, with a **Total** on the right. At the bottom, a pinned **Total** row shows the sum of every day and your full week at a glance.

## Header

The header sits above the toolbar and contains:

- The plugin title — **Weekly Logs**
- A status indicator that shows the current save state — **Draft saved**, **Auto-Saving**, or **Saved**
- The **Export** button

## Toolbar

The main controls sit in a bordered toolbar strip below the header. The toolbar contains the view toggle, week navigation, search, filters, and the Team Logs switch.

- Collapse the toolbar to save space using the collapse control on the right, or press **Ctrl+Shift+F** / **Cmd+Shift+F**.
- When collapsed, a **Toolbar** button appears next to **Export** in the header so you can expand it again.
- When filters are active, filter chips appear above the grid below the toolbar.

View mode, search, week navigation, filters, Team Logs, and Export are each covered in their own sections.

## Grid sections

The grid is divided into two sections. Each section can be collapsed independently so you can focus on what matters at any point.

### Items You Logged This Week

Everything you have already entered hours against this week. Tasks appear here once you have logged at least one entry against them.

### Proposed items

Work that has been suggested for you — tasks currently in progress and assigned to you, items carried over from last week, or items referenced from a teammate's timesheet. See [Proposed items](/plugins/weekly-logs/proposed-items) for a full breakdown.

## Cells

Each cell in the grid represents one day on one task.

- **Today** is highlighted in the column header.
- Click a cell to enter hours.
- A notes icon appears on the cell on hover — click it, or press **Space Bar**, to add a description to that entry.
- Every hour you enter stays attached to the exact task it belongs to.

## Task rows

Each task row shows:

- The **item number** and **task name**
- A **breadcrumb** showing the workstream path the task belongs to
- A **notes icon** on cells that have a description attached
- A **delete icon** in the total cell to remove all entries for that task across the week

## Auto-save

You do not need to manually save. Weekly Logs saves automatically as you work.

- Changes are saved to a local draft immediately as you type.
- Every few seconds, the draft is synced to the server in the background.
- The status indicator in the header reflects the current state — **Draft saved** first, then **Auto-Saving**, then **Saved**.
- If you close the tab mid-entry, your draft is restored when you return.
