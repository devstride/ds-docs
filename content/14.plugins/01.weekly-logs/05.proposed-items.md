title: Proposed items
description: Suggested work in Weekly Logs — Assigned, Recent, and By Person tabs.

![Weekly Logs proposed items](/images/weekly-logs/proposed-items.png)

You do not have to add every task yourself. Weekly Logs can suggest them for you.

At the bottom of the grid, below **Items You Logged This Week**, sits the **Proposed items** section. It has three tabs — **Assigned**, **Recent**, and **By Person** — each pulling in tasks from a different source.

::alert{title="Availability" type="info"}
Proposed items only appears when **Team Logs** is off (your own timesheet) and you are in **List** or **Grouped** view. It is not shown in the multi-user Team Logs layout.
::

Use the tabs on the **Proposed items** header to switch what is shown. The section itself can be collapsed like any other grid group.

## Assigned

**Assigned** shows work items that are currently in progress and assigned to you — tasks the system already knows you are working on this week.

1. Open the **Assigned** tab on **Proposed items**.
2. Review the suggested in-progress rows.
3. Click an item to move it into your timesheet.
4. Enter hours and notes on the row the same way as any other task.

## Recent

**Recent** shows items you logged time on last week. If you are working on the same tasks across multiple weeks — which most people are — this tab surfaces them so you do not have to find them again.

1. Open the **Recent** tab on **Proposed items**.
2. Review items carried over from the previous week.
3. Click an item to add it to this week's timesheet.
4. Log your hours on the row.

## By Person

**By Person** is for when you need to mirror or reference a teammate's timesheet — useful for shared tasks, pairing sessions, or coordinating entries across a team.

1. Open the **By Person** tab on **Proposed items**.
2. Click the **+** button on the tab to open the user selector.
3. Pick one or more teammates. Their logged items for the week load underneath, grouped by person.
4. Click any item to add it to your own timesheet and log time against it.

::alert{title="Editing other users' entries" type="info"}
By Person lets you reference and copy items from a teammate's timesheet. To edit another user's time entries directly, switch to **Team Logs** — see [Team Logs](/plugins/weekly-logs/team-logs).
::

## Disabled tabs

If a tab appears dimmed, there are no items available in that bucket for the current week. For example, **Assigned** will be dimmed if you have no in-progress items, and **Recent** will be dimmed if nothing was logged last week.
