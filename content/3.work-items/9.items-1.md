## Sorting in the Items Section

## Overview
The "Items" section in DevStride is where you can view and manage all the tasks and work items. Sorting these items helps in prioritizing, organizing, and finding tasks efficiently.

## How to Sort Items
1. Navigate to the "Items" section in DevStride.
2. Click on the "Sort" button, located in the top right corner above the item list.

## Available Sorting Fields
You can sort the items by the following fields:

- Date Added: Sorts items by the date and time they were added to the system.
- Start Date: Organizes items by the date they were started.
- Due Date: Arranges items by their due date, helping to prioritize urgent tasks.
- Item Type: Sorts items by their type, such as Epic, Story, Bug, etc.
- Author: Organizes items by the person who created them.
- Point Effort: Sorts items by the estimated effort required, usually in story points.
- Time Spent: Organizes items by the amount of time logged against them.
- Team: Sorts items by the team assigned to them.
- Cycle: Organizes items by the development cycle or sprint they are associated with.
- Board: Sorts items by the board they are part of.
- Subtasks Count: Organizes items by the number of subtasks they contain.
- Labels Count: Sorts items by the number of labels attached.
- Dependencies Count: Organizes items by the number of dependencies linked to them.
- Title: Sorts items alphabetically by their title.
- Assignee: Organizes items by the person assigned to work on them.
- Priority: Sorts items by their priority level.
- Status: Organizes items by their current status in the workflow.
- Estimate: Sorts items by the estimated time needed for completion.

## Applying Sorting
To apply a sorting order:
1. Select the desired field from the "By Field" dropdown menu.
2. Choose the sorting order:
   - Ascending (↑) for a top-to-bottom increase.
   - Descending (↓) for a top-to-bottom decrease.
3. Click the "Sort" button to reorganize the items list.

## Example
For instance, if you want to view the most recently added items first:
1. Open the "Sort" panel.
2. Select "Date Added" from the dropdown.
3. Set the order to "Descending".
4. Click "Sort" to apply.
