---
title: "Export timesheet data"
description: "Export Weekly Logs data to CSV for reporting and analysis."
---

You can export timesheet data for reporting or analysis outside the plugin.

## Steps

1. Click **Export** on the toolbar.
2. Enter a name for the export file.
3. Choose the **date range** to include.
4. Select any **custom fields** you want in the export.
5. Click **Export** to generate and download the CSV file.

The file can be opened in spreadsheet tools for further review or sharing.
