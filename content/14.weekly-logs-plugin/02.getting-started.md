## Open the Weekly Logs Plugin

1. Open the **Plugins** section from the menu.
2. Choose **Weekly Logs**.

The Weekly Logs interface loads in the application.

## Authentication

The plugin uses the same authentication as the DevStride application.

- You do not sign in again separately.
- Your user and organization are loaded automatically.

If something goes wrong:

- Refresh the page.
- If the session may have expired, sign out and sign back in.
