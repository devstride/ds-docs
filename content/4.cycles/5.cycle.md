# Managing Options in Cycles

Devstride's "Cycles" section provides a structured approach to managing your project's lifecycle. Use the options within this section to customize the visibility of cycle items based on your preferences.

## Accessing Cycle Options

1. From the left-hand navigation panel, select the **Cycles** section.
2. Click the `Options` button located at the top of the screen, adjacent to the `Sort` and `Export` buttons.

## Customizing Cycle Item Visibility

Within the options menu, you can manage the display of cycle items by showing or hiding different fields:

### Hidden Fields

- Activate the `Hidden Fields` toggle to reveal a list of fields that can be shown or hidden within the cycle view.
- Check or uncheck the boxes next to each field name to customize your display. Options include:
  - `Item Type`: The category of the cycle item.
  - `Parent Path`: The hierarchy or path leading to the item.
  - `Subtasks`: Any sub-items associated with the main cycle item.
  - `Dependencies`: Linked items that the cycle item depends on.
  - `Due Date`: The deadline assigned to the cycle item.
  - `Comments`: Notes or discussions attached to the item.
  - `Tags`: Labels used for categorization or filtering.
  - `Priority`: The importance or urgency level of the item.
  - `Time Spent`: The amount of work time recorded against the item.
  - `Time`: Other time-related fields.
  -  `GitHub`: Integration status or links related to GitHub repositories.

### Applying Changes

- After selecting or deselecting fields:
  - Click the `Apply` button to update your cycle items view with the new settings.

## Clearing Hidden Fields

- If you wish to quickly show all fields, you can:
  - Click the `Clear` button within the Hidden Fields menu to deselect all hidden options.
  - Press `Apply` to confirm and update the view.
