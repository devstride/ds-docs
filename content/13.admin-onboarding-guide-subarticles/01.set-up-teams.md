## 1. Setting Up Teams 

![SET_UP_TEAMS.png](/images/SET_UP_TEAMS.png)

### Step 1: Accessing Settings
1. On the left-hand side of your DevStride dashboard, click on the `Settings` tab.
2. Under the `Settings` section, select `Teams`. This is where you can manage all team-related settings.

### Step 2: Creating a New Team
1. At the top-right corner of the `Teams` section, click on the `New Team` button.
2. Fill in the team name and any other relevant details in the pop-up form.
3. Click `Save` to create the team.

### Step 3: Adding Users to Teams
1. To add users to a team, click on the team you wish to add members to.
2. Inside the team's page, click on `Invite Users`.
3. Select the users you want to add to the team and assign them a role.
4. Click `Send Invites` to add the users to the team.

### Step 4: Setting Up Team Permissions
1. In the team's settings, navigate to the `Permissions` or `Visibility` tab.
2. Choose the level of access you want team members to have. This could range from `Public` to various restricted access levels.
3. Make sure to save changes after setting the permissions.

### Step 5: Managing Team Roster
- The `Roster` section displays all members of the team.
- You can add or remove members by clicking on their profile icon.
- To manage a member's role or remove them from the team, click on the `...` next to their name and select the appropriate action.

### Step 6: Adjusting Team Visibility
- Under the `Visibility` column, you can set each team's visibility to `Public` or `Private`.
- Click on the visibility status to toggle between `Public` and `Private`.

### Step 7 : Saving Your Settings
- After making the necessary changes, ensure that you save your settings.
- Look for a `Save` or `Confirm` button within each section to make your changes permanent.

Remember to regularly review and update your team settings to reflect any changes in your organization or project requirements.
