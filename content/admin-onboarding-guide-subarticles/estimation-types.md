## Estimation Types



### Effort Point Estimation

Effort points are used for estimating the effort required for tasks.

1. Go to `Settings` > `Data Model` > `Effort Point Estimation`.
2. Enable or disable effort points for each team.

![EFFORT_POINT.png](/images/EFFORT_POINT.png)

* * *


### Time Estimation

Time estimates help in planning and tracking time for tasks.

![TIME_ESTIMATION.png](/images/TIME_ESTIMATION.png)

#### 1. Create Range

1. Go to `Settings` > `Data Model` > `Time Estimation`.
2. Click `New` to create a new time range.

#### 2. Group into Collections

1. Group time ranges into collections based on teams or projects.

#### 3. Assign Collections to Teams

1. Assign time estimation collections to specific teams.
