### Custom Fields

Custom Fields allow you to capture additional information for your items.

![CREATE_CUSTOM_FIELD.png](/images/CREATE_CUSTOM_FIELD.png)

#### 1. Create Fields

1. Go to `Settings` > `Data Model` > `Custom Fields`.
2. Click `New` to create a new custom field.
3. Specify the name and type of the custom field.

#### 2. Group into Collections

1. Create a new collection for custom fields if needed.
2. Drag and drop custom fields into collections to categorize them.

#### 3. Assign to Item Types

1. Select an item type.
2. Assign a collection of custom fields to the item type.
