Once your Workstreams, Items, and Boards are set up, **Gantt charts** give you a timeline view of the same work — useful for date-driven planning and spotting dependencies.

Gantt chart setup and usage now has its own dedicated module — see **[Track Progress: Gantts](/major-modules/track-progress-gantts/setting-up-gantt-charts)** for the full walkthrough, including navigating the timeline and the table view.
