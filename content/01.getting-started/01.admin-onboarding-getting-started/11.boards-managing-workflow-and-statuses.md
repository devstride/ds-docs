title: "Boards: Managing Workflow and Statuses"
description: "Boards are how DevStride teams execute — set up perpetual or cycle-based boards, organize working groups, and move work across statuses."
---

Once your Workstreams and items are in place, **Boards** are where teams actually execute: moving items through statuses, organizing by working group, and choosing between perpetual (ongoing) or cycle-based (time-boxed) execution.

Board setup and usage now has its own dedicated module — see **[Plan Delivery: Boards](/major-modules/boards/how-devstride-manages-the-when)** for the full walkthrough, including perpetual vs. cycle boards, working group folders, and WIP limiters.

For setting the cadence your cycle boards run on, see **Creating Cadences** next.
