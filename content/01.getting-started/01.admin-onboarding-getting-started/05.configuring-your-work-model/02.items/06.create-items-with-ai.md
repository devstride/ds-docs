---
title: "Create Items with AI"
description: "Beyond adding items by hand, you can generate a batch of items from notes or a document with AI — useful while you're populating your work model."
---

As you set up your items, it's worth knowing you don't have to create everything by hand. **Create Items with AI** turns notes, a requirements doc, or pasted text into a batch of ready-to-edit items in one pass — a fast way to populate your work model from material you already have.

You'll find it on the green **Item** button's dropdown, wherever you add items: choose **Create Items with AI**, add your text or attach a document, and review the suggested items before creating them under the location and work type you pick.

➡️ For the full walkthrough — supported file types, guiding what the AI extracts, reusable templates, and limits — see **[Using AI in DevStride → Creating Items with AI](/admin-onboarding-getting-started/using-ai-in-devstride/create-items-with-ai)**.
