## Sorting in Workstreams

## Overview
Within the "Workstreams" section of DevStride, users can organize their view by sorting both items and workstreams according to various criteria. This allows for a more efficient management of tasks and workstream structure.

## Sorting Workstreams
You can sort workstreams by the following fields:

- Rank
- Title
- Date Added
- Date Updated

### How to Sort Workstreams
1. Navigate to the "Workstreams" section.
2. Click on the "Sort" button in the upper right corner.
3. In the "Sort Workstreams" section, select a field to sort by.
4. Choose the sorting order:
   - Ascending (↑)
   - Descending (↓)
5. Click on the "Sort" button to apply the changes.

## Sorting Items
Items within a workstream can be sorted by the following fields:

- Rank
- Date Added
- Start Date
- Due Date
- Item Type
- Author
- Point Effort
- Time Spent
- Subtasks Count
- Labels Count
- Dependencies Count
- Title
- Assignee
- Estimate

### How to Sort Items
1. Inside a specific workstream, click on the "Sort" button in the upper right corner.
2. In the "Sort Items" section, select a field to sort by.
3. Choose the sorting order:
   - Ascending (↑)
   - Descending (↓)
4. Click on the "Sort" button to apply the changes.

## Example
To sort items by Title in ascending order:

1. Click on the "Sort" button.
2. Under "Sort Items", select "Title" from the dropdown menu.
3. Choose "Ascending" as the order.
4. Click "Sort" to rearrange the items.
