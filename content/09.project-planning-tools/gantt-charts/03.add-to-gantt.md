## How to Add Items to a Gantt Chart in DevStride

Adding initiatives and milestones to your Gantt chart is a straightforward process. This guide will walk you through adding both to your Gantt chart.

## Adding an Initiative

An initiative is a major effort or project that consists of multiple tasks and milestones.

### Step 1: Open the Initiative Form

- Click the `+ Add to Gantt` button located at the top right corner of the Gantt view.
- Select `Initiative` from the drop-down menu.

### Step 2: Fill in Initiative Details

- In the `NEW INITIATIVE` form that appears, enter the name of the initiative in the `NAME` field.
- Add an optional `DESCRIPTION` for the initiative.
- Choose a `COLOR` to represent the initiative on the Gantt chart.

### Step 3: Create the Initiative

- Once all details are filled in, click the `Create Initiative` button to add the initiative to the Gantt chart.

## Adding a Milestone

A milestone marks a significant point in the project timeline.

### Step 1: Open the Milestone Form

- Click the `+ Add to Gantt` button next to the `New Gantt` button.
- Choose `Milestone` from the options.

### Step 2: Fill in Milestone Details

- In the `NEW MILESTONE` form, enter a `NAME` for the milestone.
- Provide an optional `DESCRIPTION`.
- Select a `COLOR` for the milestone.
- Set the `DATE` for when the milestone is expected to be reached.

### Step 3: Create the Milestone

- Click the `Create Milestone` button to add the milestone to your Gantt chart.

## Assigning Tasks to an Initiative

You can group tasks under an initiative for better organization.

### Step 1: Select the Task(s)

- Click on the checkbox next to the task(s) you want to assign to an initiative.

### Step 2: Add to Initiative

- With the task(s) selected, click the `+ Add to Gantt` button.
- Choose `Initiative` and then `Select an initiative` from the dropdown menu that appears in the pop-up.

### Step 3: Confirm Addition

- After selecting the initiative, click on `Add Items` to assign the selected task(s) to the chosen initiative.

By following these steps, you can effectively organize your Gantt chart with initiatives and milestones in DevStride.
