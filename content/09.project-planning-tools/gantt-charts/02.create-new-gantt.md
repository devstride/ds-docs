## How to Create a New Gantt Chart in DevStride

Follow these simple steps to create and set up a new Gantt chart for your project management needs in DevStride.

## Step 1: Access the Gantt View

- On the left-hand side panel, click on the `Gantt` option under the `VIEWS` section.

## Step 2: Initiate New Gantt Chart Creation

- In the top-right corner of the Gantt view, click on the `New Gantt` button.

## Step 3: Provide Gantt Chart Details

- A form should appear prompting you to enter details for your new Gantt chart.
- Fill in the `NAME` field with a name for your Gantt roadmap.
- Optionally, add a `DESCRIPTION` for your Gantt chart for clarity and additional information.

## Step 4: Create the Gantt Chart

- Once you have filled in the details, click on the `Create Gantt` button to generate your new Gantt chart.

## Step 5: Format Your Gantt Chart

- After the creation of your Gantt chart, you can add tasks and milestones by clicking on the `Add to Gantt` button.
- Specify `Start date` and `Due date` for each task to appear on the timeline.
- Use the `Duration` field to define how long a task will take.
- Monitor progress by updating the `Progress` field as your project moves forward.

## Step 6: Save and View

- After adding all your tasks and setting their respective details, your Gantt chart will automatically save and update.
- You can now view the overall project timeline and individual task durations on the Gantt chart.

Congratulations! You have created and formatted a new Gantt chart in DevStride.
