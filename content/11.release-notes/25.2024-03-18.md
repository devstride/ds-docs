## Enhanced Cycle Cadences and Estimation Tools

We're rolling out another update to DevStride, designed to refine your project planning and execution with improved estimation controls and more intuitive user interfaces. Here's the lowdown on the latest features and fixes:

## What's New?

- **Item-Level Estimation Configuration**: Shifting gears for greater precision, point and time estimations can now be set at the item type level, granting you finer control over your project planning processes.
  
- **Cadence Management Enhancements**: We've expanded your toolkit with the ability to update and archive your cadences, timeboxes, and cycles, ensuring your project timelines stay in sync with your evolving needs.
  
- **Improved Cadence Settings Navigation**: Scroll through your cadence settings with ease, thanks to new scroll buttons that enhance viewability and accessibility.

## Fixes and Improvements

- **Live Update Reliability**: Addressed and fixed an intermittent issue with live updates to ensure consistent, real-time information flow across your projects.

- **Keyboard Navigation Optimized**: Work item tables and general selectors have been fine-tuned for keyboard interaction, making for a smoother, more accessible navigation experience for all users.

With these updates, we aim to streamline your project management even further, giving you the power and flexibility to manage your projects with confidence. As always, your feedback is invaluable to us as we continue to improve DevStride, so please reach out if you have any suggestions or need support.
