Item Position Control in Map

#### - What's New?

When adding a new item or Workstream via the quick-add popup, you can now choose whether to insert it at the top or bottom of the map. A new checkbox gives you this control, and your preference is saved for the session.

![image.png](/images/image289.png)

The map will also auto-scroll to the newly added item if the list is long.

#### - Why It Matters

Some lists (like step-by-step guides) are more intuitive top-to-bottom. Others (like bug reports or requests) benefit from the most recent items at the top. This update gives you the flexibility to work your way.
