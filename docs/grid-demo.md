# Weekly Logs — Grid

[▶ Watch Demo](grid-demo.mp4)

---

The grid is the core of Weekly Logs. Every task you are tracking sits as a row, and every day of the week is a column — Monday through Sunday, with a Total on the right. At the bottom, a pinned Total row shows the sum of every day and your full week at a glance.

The grid is divided into two sections. **Items You Logged This Week** shows everything you have already entered hours against. Below that, **Proposed Items** surfaces work that is assigned to you or carried over from the previous week. We will cover this in detail in its own demo. Each section is collapsible, so you can focus on what matters at any point.

Each cell represents one day on one task. Click a cell to enter hours.

If you need to add context to an entry, use the notes button that appears on the cell. It opens a small popup where you can add a description alongside the hours.

Every hour you enter stays attached to the task it belongs to. That is what makes the grid the foundation for everything else in Weekly Logs.
