# Weekly Logs — Proposed Items

[▶ Watch Demo](proposed-items-demo.mp4)

---

You don't have to add every task yourself. Weekly Logs can suggest them for you.

At the bottom of the grid, there's a section called **Proposed Items**. It has three tabs: **Assigned**, **Recent**, and **By Person**.

**Assigned** shows work items that are currently in progress and assigned to you or your team — tasks the system already knows you're working on. Click an item and it moves into your timesheet. Log your hours. Done. No searching, no copy-pasting item numbers.

**Recent** shows items you logged time on last week. If you're working on the same tasks across multiple weeks — which most people are — this tab surfaces them so you don't have to find them again. Click an item to carry it forward into this week's timesheet.

**By Person** is for when you need to mirror or reference someone else's timesheet. Click the **+** button on the tab. A user selector opens. Pick one or more teammates. Their logged items for the week load underneath, grouped by person. You can then click any of their items to add it to your own timesheet and log time. Useful for shared tasks, pairing sessions, or when you're coordinating time entries across a team.
