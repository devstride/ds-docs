# Weekly Logs — Search

[▶ Watch Demo](search-demo.mp4)

---

Finding the right work should not slow you down. 

- Weekly Logs gives you two ways to add items to your timesheet: a quick keyword search for when you know what you are looking for, and an advanced hierarchy browser for when you need to explore.

- The search bar sits in the toolbar. Type at least two characters — a ticket number, a title, or a keyword — and results appear immediately. Items already in your timesheet are marked. Click a result, and it lands in the grid, ready for a time entry.

- Before searching, you can narrow the scope using the workstream filter next to the search bar. Select a workstream, and results are limited to items within that folder.

- For times when you need to browse rather than search, use the advanced search button — the folder icon next to the search bar. This opens the hierarchy browser.

- The left side shows your full item hierarchy — workstreams, and work items. Navigate into a folder using the breadcrumb, and select the items that belong in your week. Click **Add Selected** and all items are added to the grid at once.
