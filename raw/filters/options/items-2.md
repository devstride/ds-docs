# Items

> 

## Managing Options in Items

Devstride's "Items" section offers a detailed list of all tasks and items within your project. Customize this list to better track and manage your project's elements according to your workflow needs.

## Accessing Item Options

1. Click on the **Items** section from the left-hand navigation menu.
2. Locate the `Options` button near the top-right corner, next to the `Sort` and `Export` buttons, and click on it.
3. From the drop-down, choose `Table columns` to modify the visible columns in the items list.

## Customizing Item Visibility

The "Manage Columns" dialog allows you to control the visibility of various item details:

### Available Fields

- The fields available for display customization include:

  - `Item Type`: The classification of the item (e.g., Story, Epic).
  - `Cycle / Board`: The cycle or board that the item is associated with.
  - `Due Date`: The expected completion date for the item.
  - `Start Date`: The date when work on the item began.
  - `Point Estimate`: The estimated size or complexity of the item.
  - `Time Estimate`: The amount of time estimated to complete the item.
  - `Time Spent`: The actual time recorded against the item.
  - `Priority`: The urgency or importance of the item.
  - `Tags`: Identifiers for quick categorization and filtering.
  - `Release`: The release that the item is a part of.
  - `For Customer`: Indicates if the item is directly related to a customer.
  - `Blocked Reason`: The reason why an item may be blocked.

### Applying Your Customization

- To apply the changes:

  - Check or uncheck the boxes next to each field to toggle their visibility.
  - Once you've set your preferences, click the `Apply` button to update the items list.

## Resetting to Default View

If you want to revert back to the standard view with all fields visible:

- Open the "Manage Columns" dialog.
- Click on the `Clear` button to deselect all hidden fields.
- Hit `Apply` to see all the information associated with your items.
