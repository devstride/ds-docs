# Status & WIP Limiters

> 

### Statuses & WIP Limiters

Statuses track the progress of items, and WIP limiters help manage work in progress.

![STATUSES_WIP.png](/images/STATUSES_WIP.png)

#### 1. Create Status

1. Go to `Settings` > `Data Model` > `Statuses & WIP Limiters`.
2. Click `New` to create a new status.
3. Choose a color and set WIP limits if necessary.

#### 2. Group into Collections

1. Group statuses into collections for better organization.

#### 3. Assign Collections to Teams

1. Assign status collections to different teams to use within their workflows.
